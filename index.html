About Us

Fluttuo was born with the desire to offer a fresh alternative to traditional jewellery, which could suit the uniqueness of contemporary women. Fluttuo constantly experiments with new materials and innovative designs. All jewels are made once only. They are nickel free, hand-produced in Italy and carefully packaged for your enjoyment.
